War War

The Most Mentioned Game Of Global Game Jam HK 2013

Our hero WARWAR, has achieved everything he could do in the game world. What he only wants to do is to die and get free. Since he is so blessed by the god, he can not die in the game world. Your goal is to help him achieve his final mission -- Death. This is a pixel-style adventure game developed by a group of five, including two programmers, two designers and a sound effect artist. 5 levels were built intensively within 48 hours during the Global Game Jam 2013 HK.
