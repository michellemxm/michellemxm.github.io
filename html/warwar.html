WAR WAR

Category:
MDes Capstone Project
Wearable/Tactile Interaction Design

Date:
Jan - Jul 2014

Institute:
MDes Interaction Design
School of Design, HKPU

The story:
Our hero WARWAR, has achieved everything he could do in the game world. What he only wants to do is to die and get free. Since he is so blessed by the god, he can not die in the game world. Your goal is to help him achieve his final mission -- Death.
This is a pixel-style adventure game developed by a group of five, including two programmers, two designers and a sound effect artist. 5 levels were build intensively within 48 hours during the Global Game Jam 2013 HK.
 
Project Team
Developer: Jing LIAO | Ka Yee LAW
Game Designer: Michelle MA | Renee SIU
Sound Artist: Xia HUA
 
Awarded as 'The most mentioned game' in GGJ 2013 HK
