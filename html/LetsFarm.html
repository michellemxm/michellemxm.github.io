Let's Farm

Category:
Commercial Project
Illustration

Date:
Jun - Aug 2013

Client:
Playday Games Ltd. Hong Kong

Here is a set of illustrations and animations I created for the Android Mobile Game developed by Playday Games Limited Hong Kong. I participated in the early design and development of this game as a design intern in summer 2013.

In the BETA version, I was in charge of the illustrations of the food series, drafting animals on the farm and creating the animations. I also contributed to the game concept design and the interaction design, including part of the user interface.

Check it out on Google Play：
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=letsfarm.com.playday&hl=en

View other games produced by Playday Games：
https://play.google.com/store/apps/developer?id=Playday+Games
