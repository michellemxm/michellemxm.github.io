Just We
The Concept

 
Lovers, close friends, families like to purchase accessories in pairs or sets to show their relationship. People often feel shy to express their care and love while they may feel rather difficult to make apology after arguing. This is especially the case when they are in a close relationship with each other.We were inspired and therefore designed a pair of illuminating emotion communication accessories for expressing their feelings to each other.

 
Parrot is a kind of bird that has the ability to imitate people talking. Here it acts as the role of courier and deliver the messages.

The Series
Product Family for Different User Groups

The use scenario of JustWE is not limited to lovers and couples only. Rather than that, you may purchase it as a gift to all people that you care about, such as your family members, your best friends, etc.

How
it words

JustWE works in pairs: the boy holds the blue parrot while the girl will have the pink one at hand. The control switches of the blue locate on the pink parrot. When user expresses his/her emotion by switching on one light, red for example, the red LED on the other parrot would be illuminating. RGB LEDs are used in this case, which enable JustWE to illume in color gradient. Bluetooth is employed for signal tranmission.
 

 
 
The color of red, yellow and blue have been chosen to denote negative, neutral and positive feelings accordingly. It has been always mentioned that the eyes are the windows of the soul. Hereby, the illuminating LEDs are installed in the parrot’s eyes.
