Versatile&Expandable
Multiple power solutions for optimum utilization
 
 

Adopting MicroSD card design, Smart Tag is geared for myriad practical applications. It is compatible with different types of card readers and card slots. The Box Series Beacons, which employ Tag as the PCB, provide Sensoro Tag with different battery solutions.

Smart 4AA Beacon Sensor and USB Beacon are currently available to the market. 4AA, powered by four AA batteries, is capable to provide stable functionality for over 500 continuous days. USB Beacon will be mostly used at places that have usb chargers or computers.
