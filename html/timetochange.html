This project looked into Hong Kong's current glass recycle issues. Research results indicate that over 250 tons of glass waste are produced veryday, merely less than 5% of which are recycled. Disposal channels would mostly be landfill, which led us to another problem - limited and meanwhile decreasing landfill soil we have in Hong Kong.
The team designed and developed an awareness campaign for Hong Kong residents from glass product suppliers, vendors to end consumers, encouraging people to their parts in glass recycle. The campaign system involves branding, recycle bin redesign, accreditation and graphic advertisements.
