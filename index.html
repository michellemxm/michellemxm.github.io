PORTFOLIO

VISION

Integrity

The nature of a design has nothing to do with how it looks like. A good design is always honest and it respects the nature of the object itself. It does not manipulate the customer with promises that are beyond what it can do.

Unobtrusive

A design, either tangible product or intangible system, serves as a tool, which is neither a decoration nor a piece of pure art. Therefore, the design should be neutral and restrained, leaving room for the customer’s self-expression.

Innovative

Even a traditional object looks outrageous when it first appears. Although the development of technologies provides more opportunities to design, a designer’s way of thinking should not be technology-dependent.

Social Responsible

A good design should be of social value and make user’s life better in some way. Something grows in value the more it is used, or become indispensable companion through years of usage, is the kind of design I am striving to achieve.

PROFILE

About Michelle

Michelle is an interaction and industrial designer who has just graduated from MDes Interaction Design, School of Design, the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. She received her Bachelor of Engineering in Product Analysis and Engineering Design from the Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2013.
With enthusiasm and passion towards design, Michelle, who lived in Beijing, started her design education in PolyU in 2009. During the last four years, Michelle has been trained and enhanced in design professional skills and design thinking. Her work encompasses theory and practice of industrial design, product engineering, communication design, graphic/visual design, software/hardware interaction and user experience design.
