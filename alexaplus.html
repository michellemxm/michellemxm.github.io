Alexa+ in Mobile App

Overview

Alexa+ is Amazon’s next-generation evolution of Alexa, powered by generative AI to deliver more natural conversations, personalized interactions, and autonomous task execution. On mobile, it plays a key role in increasing DAA/MAA by better supporting daily habitual actions and meeting the needs of core groups like smart-home users, while the business focuses on elevating the conversational experience and empowering customers with LLM-driven intelligence. It marks a major leap in how people engage with Alexa across devices—from mobile and smart speakers to displays and the web.

My Role

As the Design Lead for Alexa+ on the Alexa mobile app, I shape how these new AI capabilities manifest within one of Alexa’s most versatile and widely used touchpoints. I partner closely with design leads across Echo devices, web, and Fire TV to ensure coherence across endpoints, while driving the evolution of core mobile experiences and integrating AI-driven enhancements that raise usability and customer value. Beyond execution, I contribute to design planning and product strategy, helping define the long-term vision and roadmap for how Alexa+ expands its role in people’s daily lives.

Design Evolution

Throughout the development process, we iterated on multiple design concepts to arrive at the final solution. Each iteration brought us closer to understanding the core user needs and technical requirements.

Our initial concept focused on creating a seamless integration between AI assistance and traditional development workflows, emphasizing clarity and ease of use.

The wireframe phase allowed us to explore different interaction patterns and information architecture approaches, testing various layouts with our target users.

The final design represents a refined balance of functionality and aesthetics, incorporating user feedback and technical constraints into a cohesive experience.
