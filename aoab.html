OVERVIEW

Introduction to AOAB Project

      The objective of the Service Design, Brand and Value Creation project is to improve the experience of Art on the Atlanta Beltline. The Art on the Beltline exhibition is a year-round art program exhibiting multi-genre artwork along the Beltline trails. Over 1.7 million users have visited the trails. The Art & Culture program now focuses on addressing the following questions:

        - How can awareness of the artwork be achieved in a cohesive manner and without disrupting transit?
        - What possibilities are there for people to interact, learn and enjoy while visiting the BeltLine and the Art on the BeltLine exhibition?
        - How can art contribute to placemaking and how may it contribute to showing what a specific neighborhood, or Atlanta, is all about?
        - In what ways can neighbors and BeltLine adjacent communities and their resources be activated to promote arts and culture?

      We here present our project AOAB(Art On the Atlanta Beltline) Information Platform, which we designed, prototyped to address some of the questions above. A series of touchpoint combining both physical and digital formats were designed and tested. The platform provides an efficient channel for ABI administrators, approved artists, and adjacent business owner to co-edit and manage the information that is shown to the visitor. The goal is to provide context-aware, accurate and the most updated information to the visitors with minimal labor and resources.
